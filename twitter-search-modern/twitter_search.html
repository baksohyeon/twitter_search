Twitter Search Syntax Guide:

  • from:username - Tweets written by this account
  • to:username - Tweets sent to this account
  • since:YYYY-MM-DD - Tweets after this date
  • until:YYYY-MM-DD - Tweets before this date
  • min_retweets:number - Tweets with at least this many retweets
  • filter:nativeretweets - Only show retweets
